about me

WINNIE ZHANG is a currently a 2nd year student at George Brown College for Interaction Design and Development. Working in freelance photography and design, she is able to create and present meaningful content for her clients while engaging with the audience. As a content creator on her personal Instagram, she’s completed brand collaborations for various clients.

she is well immersed in programs such as Autodesk Maya, Adobe Creative Suite and Atom / Visual Studio Code (html,css,javascript). with years of experience as a freelance photographer, she has the skills to create and present meaningful and well designed content to her audiences while staying on top of trends.

her professional experience includes working as a content creator on her personal instagram with brand collaborations for various clients such as Mejuri, The Body Shop, P&G etc. winnie has worked the role of social media coordinator, at Sorry Coffee / Kit and Ace, where she managed the cafe’s social media platforms and organized events and workshops.
