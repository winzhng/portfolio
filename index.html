about me

My name is Winnie

I am designer, photographer and content creator. I'm currently studying Interaction Design & Development at George Brown College, and have an array of skills in graphic design, animation, front end Development while working with programs such as Adobe Creative Suite, Autodesk Maya, Invision, Marvel and Microsoft Office Suite.

As a content creator on my personal Instagram and experience as a freelance photographer, I'm able to curate original and trending content by staging photos with good composition. My consistency and aesthetic has provided many opportunities to work on brand collaborations for various clients.

my work

Branding for Cafe Concept - Magnolia Cafe

I created a mobile order app for a conceptual cafe called, Magnolia. The app features a landing page, home page, rewards and payment page, menu and ordering page and profile. The inspiration of the name ties back to my Chinese heritage where Magnolias are believed to symbolize longevity, beauty and perseverance. For the colour palette, I chose to stick with earth and neutral tones.
