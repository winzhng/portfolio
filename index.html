my work

UI Branding for Fictitious Companies

Magnolia Cafe: I created a mobile ordering app for a conceptual cafe called, Magnolia. The app features a landing page, home page, rewards and payment page, menu and ordering page and profile. The inspiration of the name ties back to my Chinese heritage where Magnolias are believed to symbolize longevity, beauty and perseverance. For the colour palette, I chose to stick with earth and neutral tones.

Breeze: A mobile webpage for a fictitious sustainable skincare and wellness brand called, Breeze. The mockups I designed features a home page, product list and two product pages with descriptions. Self-care and wellness has become increasely important during the pandemic, to take a deep breathe, relax and take care of yourself mentally and physically. Breeze promotes self-love, positivity, ethical and sustainable pratices.

UI Branding for Fictitious Companies

Lunar: This is a web page for a home decor brand called, Lunar. The company offers high-end home decor, I chose a minimalistic and slight editorial approach compared to many companies such as West Elm, Wayfair, Ikea etc.

Flux: A magazine mock up for Flux. Flux has a minimalistic, modern style that releases bi-monthly issues that features travel tips and information of different cities around the world.
